Who We Are

About Ocean FG

Ocean FG SDN. BHD. (Company Registration Number: 1330388-P) is a prominent Malaysian Bumiputera company specializing in high-quality marine services in Kuantan, Pahang. Our primary focus is delivering top-notch marine operations across Malaysia.

As a regional leader in marine/Oil & Gas solutions, Ocean FG excels in integrating international standards with extensive local expertise. We are committed to offering efficient and superior services, leveraging our deep experience in marine logistics and agency.

Furthermore, Ocean FG manages the Sangkar Cahaya jetty and shipyard located in Kuala Pahang, Pekan. Our dedication to quality and proficiency ensures that we deliver services in the most effective manner possible.
