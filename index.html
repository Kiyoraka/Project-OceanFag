About Ocean FG

Ocean FG SDN. BHD. (Company Registration Number: 1330388-P) is a prominent Malaysian Bumiputera company specializing in high-quality marine services in Kuantan, Pahang. Our primary focus is delivering top-notch marine operations across Malaysia.

As a regional leader in marine/Oil & Gas solutions, Ocean FG excels in integrating international standards with extensive local expertise. We are committed to offering efficient and superior services, leveraging our deep experience in marine logistics and agency.

Furthermore, Ocean FG manages the Sangkar Cahaya jetty and shipyard located in Kuala Pahang, Pekan. Our dedication to quality and proficiency ensures that we deliver services in the most effective manner possible.

Geophysical Survey Services

We provide comprehensive geophysical survey services tailored to offshore projects, leveraging cutting-edge technology, skilled teams, and proven methodologies to deliver accurate and actionable subsurface data for diverse industries.

Our Geophysical Services

Our geophysical survey services are designed to meet the demanding needs of offshore and marine projects. We specialize in:

  • Seismic Surveys: High-resolution 2D, 3D, and 4D seismic surveys for subsurface mapping and reservoir characterization.
  • Magnetic and Gravity Surveys: Identifying geological structures and potential hazards.
  • Site Investigation Surveys: Detailed geophysical data for offshore construction, pipeline routing, and installation projects.
  • Unexploded Ordnance (UXO) Detection: Advanced magnetometer and side-scan sonar surveys to identify and assess potential hazards.
  • Sub-Bottom Profiling: Examining sediment layers and buried infrastructure with precision acoustic systems.
